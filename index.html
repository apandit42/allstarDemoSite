Our recipe.

The best bioengineers aren't born or built - they're evolved.

At Aminoo Biologics we know nature has already produced the best bioengineering on the planet. Powered by 3.8 billion years of evolution, life's natural biomolecules and biosystems hold the key to the next revolution in therapeutics, diagnostics, and manufacturing.

That's why we've built a generalized directed evolution platform, harnessing nature's potential to create customer defined biosensors and biomolecules. Using our own custom in vitro TX-TL emulsion system we can evolve new protein products for diagnostics and biosynthesis pathways for virtually any target, and fast.

Our system allows us to circle the design-build-test cycle faster than in vivo or screening-based methods, giving us an unmatched edge on our competition. Interested? Let's talk.
